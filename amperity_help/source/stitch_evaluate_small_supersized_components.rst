.. tooltip-stitch-small-supersized-components-start

This test computes a count of how many "small" (< 1000 recod) supersized components there are in Stitch output. Counter-intuitively, because of how Stitch works, a large count implies many records were not compared to each other, and thus records may have under-clustered.
