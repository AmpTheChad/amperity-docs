Segments A-Z

About segments

A segment defines an audience for use with a campaign.

Example

Build audiences that align to your marketing goals.

AND vs. OR

Use AND within segments to make audiences smaller. Use OR to make audiences larger.

Attributes

Use standard, predicted, and custom attributes to build segments for your campaigns.

Databases and tables

Use any database or table to build audiences in the Segment Editor.

Segment insights

How many customers? Are they active? How much did they spend?

Segment overview

View highlights for the most important details about your segments.

SQL Segment Editor

Optional. Use Presto SQL to build audiences for use with your campaigns.

Standard output

Your source of truth for how your customers interact with your brand. Updated daily.

Campaigns A-Z

About campaigns

A campaign combines segments, audiences, and destinations.

Example

Choose audiences. Define sub-audiences. Send campaigns.

Audience lists

An audience list contains all customers who are eligible to be part of a campaign.

Control groups

A subset of holdout customers; use them to measure lift in your campaigns.

Exclusion lists

A list of customers who should not be part of a campaign.

One-time campaigns

A one-time campaign is configured to be sent only once.

Recipient groups

A subset of customers within a sub-audience; market to these customers.

Recurring campaigns

A recurring campaign sends refreshed audiences on a rolling schedule.

Sub-audiences

A group of customers who will receive specific messaging.

Timing & launch dates

Tell Amperity when to send the audience and when the campaign is launched.

Destinations A-Z

About destinations

A destination is the location to which your campaigns are sent.

Example

Configure audiences and sub-audiences. Send them to any destination.

Select destination(s)

Select the destination(s) to which each recipient group will be sent.

Edit attributes

Select the attributes that are sent to each destination.

Delivery summary

Review how Amperity will send data to each destination.
